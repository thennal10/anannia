Certification Program in Domiciliary Care

In association with Pallium India, we are conducting a training program for domiciliary care. We are aiming to train SSLC qualified young adults in the basics of home nursing to bring them into this field rich with job opportunities and social impact. The course, which includes scientifically validated practical training, is for two months with a fee of Rs. 5000. The course starts on May 15th, and the last date to apply is May 5th.
