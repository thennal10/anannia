Because queer rights matter.

The Center for Gender and Sexuality is a project meant to fight for civil rights and social acceptance of the LGBTQI+ community. Our work includes organizing large queer events such as the Kerala Transgender Sports Meetup, help individual members in the community like Adam Harry, India's first transman pilot, and campaigning for foundational legislative changes, such as the Kerala Transgender Policy.
