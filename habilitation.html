Centre for Habilitation (Chilla) envisages transforming the lives of people with autism and other developmental disorders from marginalised and financially backward communities through a free and comprehensive programme.

Chilla provides educational training and occupational therapy to empower these individuals, helping them discover their strengths, develop vocational skills, and integrate into professional workplaces. The comprehensive four-year course not only equips them with essential life skills but also instills confidence, fostering a more fulfilling life.
