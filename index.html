What we are

A unique initiative, initiated by a conglomerate of social activists, social workers and other professionals with an intention to formulate activities and programs for the betterment of society, especially the various marginalized and stigmatized sections of communities in Kerala. Anannia was started in the year 2007, to be that platform through which new and innovative ventures can be set up, promoted, nurtured thus evolving sincere partakers towards the fulfilment of the vision of the organization.

Campaigns

Smartphone Donation Drive

The digital divide is a stark reality facing the education sector of Kerela, as a significant portion of the children do not have access to any digital device necessary for attending online classes. In order to bridge the digital gap, we are conducting a donation drive to supply underpriviledged children with smartphones, costing roughly ₹7500. Every donation, no matter how small, helps in the cause. Donations of old but functional smartphones, tablets, laptops, etc. are also welcome.

Arivu

Arivu is a library based at Chilla that is mainly being utilized by our children. Containing over a 1000 books, the catalogue of Arivu is varied and comprehensive, from classic novels to picture books. Our goal is to maintain a diverse collection of literature required to support the needs and complement the growth of our children. The creation of a virtual library is also in the works.
